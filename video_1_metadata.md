## 4. Thumbnail Concept Ideas (To edit in CapCut or design)

A thumbnail is the most critical factor for CTR. Here are two concepts you can generate or edit:

### Concept A: The Glowing Portal (Mysterious & Cinematic)
* **Visual**: A high-contrast photo of the Svalbard Vault entrance at night with bright green/blue lights glowing from the portal. The background is a dark, starry sky and snow.
* **Text Overlay**: "HUMANITY'S BACKUP" in large, bold, sans-serif font (Yellow or White text with a black shadow).
* **Composition**: The entrance is placed on the left third, with the text on the right third.

### Concept B: The Ice Vault (Cold & Technical)
* **Visual**: An interior shot of icy, frosted shelves holding storage boxes, with a spotlight highlighting a single box labeled with a country's flag.
* **Text Overlay**: "DOOMSDAY PREP?" or "INSIDE THE VAULT".
