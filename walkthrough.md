## What to Do Next

To start producing your video:
1. **Record the audio**: Speak the voiceover script in [video_1_script.md](file:///c:/Users/leone/OneDrive/Desktop/Vito/Work/YouTube/YT_Project/video_1_script.md) into your microphone with a calm, paced tone.
2. **Transcribe**: Upload the recording to **TurboScribe** to review your timing.
3. **Generate Visuals**: Use the Pollinations.AI prompt suggestions from the script to generate high-quality visual scenes.
4. **Edit in CapCut**: Merge your voice track, the generated video clips, and smooth transitions, and use CapCut's auto-caption feature to place text on screen.
