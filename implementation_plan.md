> [!IMPORTANT]
> To monetize a YouTube channel through the YouTube Partner Program (YPP), you currently need:
> 1. **1,000 subscribers** with **4,000 valid public watch hours** in the last 12 months, OR
> 2. **1,000 subscribers** with **10 million valid public Shorts views** in the last 90 days.
> Consistent formatting, strong hooks, and SEO optimization from Day 1 are critical to achieving this.

### Phase 2: Video Scripting and Timestamp Structuring

We will develop a highly engaging, structured script for your first video. The script will follow a proven engagement formula:
1. **The Hook (0:00 - 0:30)**: Instantly capture attention, state the value proposition, and prevent early drop-off.
2. **The Intro (0:30 - 1:00)**: Introduce the creator, set expectations, and introduce the core problem.
3. **The Core Content / Body (1:00 - 8:00)**: Break down the main points with clean pacing, visuals/B-roll cues, and narrative progression.
4. **Call to Action / Outro (8:00 - 9:00)**: Suggest another video to watch to keep viewers on your channel (boosting session watch time) and invite subscriptions.

We will provide a double-column or structured markdown script mapping:
- **Timestamp / Duration**: When each section starts and how long it lasts.
- **Narrator Script**: The exact words spoken.
- **Visuals / B-Roll / SFX**: Instructions for video editing, graphics, and sound effects.

## Verification Plan

We will verify the quality and completeness of the assets through the following steps:

### Content Quality & Script Verification
- Ensure the script length aligns with the targeted video duration (approx. 130–150 words per minute of speech).
- Verify that timestamp intervals are logical and cover all necessary transitions.
- Read through the script to test natural phrasing and flow.

### Metadata SEO Validation
- Cross-reference tags and titles against high-volume, low-competition terms in the chosen niche.
- Validate that the description formatting matches YouTube's structural guidelines for rich search snippets.
